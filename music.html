Cage The Elephant - Melophobia

Cage the Elephant is an American rock band formed in 2006, which currently consists of Matt Shultz , Brad Shultz (his older brother), Nick Bockrath, Matthan Minster, Daniel Tichenor and Jared Champion. Their music was influenced by classic rock, '90s alternative, blues, punk rock, and funk music.

Melophobia is their third album, and despite the meaning of the title, Shultz has declared in an interview with Billboard that Melophobia is not a fear of music, rather, "It's a fear of creating music under false pretenses, catering to cool, or writing to project some sort of image that's based upon social standards"
